About

This index is a collection of odd license plates I've encountered over the last few years between San Francisco and New England.

Though license plates are primarily functional, the limited customization the DMV allows lets drivers express themselves in personal and creative ways. A lot can be made of the 7 letters and numbers, which I’ve found worth capturing. This project felt like an appropriate time to show some of them.

All photos are taken by Alex Infield between 2022 and 2023. My collection is larger and continues to grow.
